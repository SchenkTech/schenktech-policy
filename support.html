Fulcrum - Support

Thanks for playing Fulcrum! If you need help or want to report an issue, we're here for you.

Common Questions

  • How do I undo a stone placement? — Two-finger tap during the placing phase to remove the last stone.
  • What are Fulcrum Gems? — Each level awards a Raw Stone, Polished, or Diamond gem based on how quickly you solve it.
  • How do hints work? — Earn points by completing levels. You can spend points to unlock hints on harder levels.
  • Is my progress saved? — Yes, your progress is saved automatically on your device.
  • Does Fulcrum require an internet connection? — No. An internet connection is only needed for Game Center leaderboards.

Report a Bug or Request a Feature
